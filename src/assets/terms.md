# Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 16, 2026

This document constitutes a formal agreement between **Caden Erwin Tutoring** ("the Tutor") and the student and/or the student's legal guardian ("the Client"). By booking a session or using the services provided by Caden Erwin Tutoring, the Client agrees to the following terms.

# I. TERMS OF SERVICE

## 1. Scope of Tutoring Services

Tutoring services are provided exclusively for the following subjects. Requests for subjects outside of this list will not be honored:

- Integrated Math 1, 2, and 3 (Freshman through Junior levels)
- Chemistry
- Pre-AP Biology (Strictly on-demand/by request only; subject to availability)

---

## 2. Service Cost

**Free of Charge:**
All tutoring sessions provided by Caden Erwin Tutoring are offered as a free service to the community. No payment, tips, donations, or fees are required or accepted.

---

## 3. Scheduling and Availability

**Booking:**
All sessions must be booked through the official booking calendar.

**Hours:**
Current operating hours are reflected solely on the booking calendar and are subject to change based on the Tutor’s academic and extracurricular schedule at Beaumont High School.

**Late Arrival:**
If a student is more than 15 minutes late, the session may be canceled at the Tutor's discretion to respect the Tutor's time and schedule.

---

## 4. Cancellation Policy

**Notice Period:**
Clients are requested to provide at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling.

**No-Shows:**
Although the service is free, the Tutor’s time is limited and valuable. Repeated failure to attend scheduled sessions without prior notice may result in permanent termination of tutoring services for that Client.

---

## 5. Academic Integrity Policy

Caden Erwin Tutoring is committed to the highest standards of academic honesty.

The Tutor will provide:

- Explanation
- Conceptual guidance
- Skill-building support
- Problem-solving strategies

The Tutor will **NOT**:

- Complete graded homework assignments
- Write essays or lab reports
- Take quizzes, exams, or online assessments on behalf of a student
- Engage in any activity that violates school or district academic integrity policies

The student is solely responsible for their own academic work, grades, and standing.

---

## 6. Limitation of Liability

Caden Erwin Tutoring strives to support academic improvement; however:

- No guarantees are made regarding specific grades, test scores, or academic outcomes.
- The Tutor is not liable for academic disciplinary actions taken by a school or educational institution.
- The Client assumes full responsibility for how tutoring guidance is used.

---

## 7. Conduct

Respectful and appropriate behavior is required at all times.

The Tutor reserves the right to:

- Terminate a session immediately if a student is disrespectful, disruptive, or inappropriate.
- Refuse future service if conduct expectations are repeatedly violated.


# II. PRIVACY POLICY & DATA PROTECTION

## 1. FERPA Compliance & Confidentiality

Caden Erwin Tutoring operates in alignment with the standards of the **Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)**.

Caden Erwin Tutoring recognizes the importance of protecting student education records and personal information.

**Non-Disclosure Policy:**
Student information, academic progress, communications, and session notes will **not** be shared, sold, disclosed, or distributed to any third parties, individuals, or organizations under any circumstances, unless required by law.

All information remains strictly between:

- The Tutor
- The Student
- The Student’s Legal Guardian (if applicable)

---

## 2. Information We Collect

To provide tutoring services, the following information may be collected:

**Client Identity**

- Student name
- Parent/guardian name (if applicable)

**Contact Information**

- School email address (@beaumontusd.k12.ca.us)
- Personal email address (e.g., @icloud.com)
- Grade level

**Academic Information**

- Current course level
- Areas of academic difficulty
- Assignments, worksheets, syllabi, or materials voluntarily provided by the Client

Only information necessary for tutoring purposes is collected.

---

## 3. Use of Information

Collected information is used solely for:

- Scheduling and confirming appointments
- Preparing customized lesson plans
- Providing tutoring support
- Sending session reminders
- Sharing relevant follow-up resources

Information is never used for advertising, marketing, or commercial resale.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

**Communication & Devices:**
Sessions and communications may utilize professional-grade hardware including a MacBook Air for digital whiteboarding and lesson preparation.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect digital communications and stored information.

**BUSD Communication Preference:**
For students within the Beaumont Unified School District, use of the district-issued email address is preferred to maintain transparency and a documented communication record.

---

## 5. Third-Party Tools

Scheduling and communication tools (such as Google Calendar) may be used for appointment management.

These third-party platforms operate under their own privacy policies. Clients are encouraged to review those policies independently.
